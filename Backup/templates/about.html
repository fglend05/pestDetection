What is Pest Detection?

Go Back

The project aims to develop a web-based mobile application for local farmers to manage agricultural crops by detecting and classifying pests and diseases. It utilizes image processing technology with CNN algorithms, specifically improving architectures like YOLO, ResNet, and VGG for better detection and classification. The application consists of modules such as information, detection and classification, results, e-Agricultural manuals, history, and settings. Limitations include a focus on specific crops, reliance on image quality for accuracy, compatibility with high-quality mobile devices, and limitations in online functionality. These limitations present areas for future improvement.
